POLEWORK BENEFITS

Pole work is good for your horses because it can help them improve their fitness, flexibility, balance, rhythm, coordination, and strength.

Pole work exercises can also make your training sessions more fun and varied, and challenge your horse mentally and physically.

Pole work exercises can strengthen and engage your horse’s back, hindquarters, and topline, which are essential for power, speed, and agility.

This can also help your horse develop a better posture and carriage, and prevent back problems

Pole work exercises can develop your horse’s stride length and quality, which can improve their performance in jumping, dressage, or eventing.

RIDER SAFETY

Equestrians should wear safety gear to prevent or minimize injury when they fall off their horse.

Nothing can prevent them from falling, but with the right equipment, they can prevent or minimize injury when they do fall off. Horse riding safety equipment includes a helmet, body protector, safety vest, and more.

Wearing correct horse riding safety equipment can mitigate the risk of injury when participating in high-impact equestrian sports like an eventing competition. A severe fall can even lead to death1.

Horse riding safety vests and other safety items are not cheap, but investing in

them is worth it as they can make a huge difference to your riding safety and enjoyment.
